The Autism Awareness Team aims to help people understand and gain further knowledge on the topic of Autism. Spreading awareness is our job - after all, "Autism Awareness" is our middle name (literally!!). 

 Ok, so first of all - what even is autism? Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental disability, which occurs due to some differences in the brain. This affects areas of interactions, communication, learning and behaviour. You can visit our "What is Autism" page for more information (link located on the navigator at the very top of this page).
