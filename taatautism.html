What is Autism..?

 

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental disability, which occurs due to some differences in the brain. This affects areas of interactions, communication, learning and behaviour.

Some common behaviours of people with autism (not all!!);

  • Over or under sensitivity to touch , sound and/or smell
